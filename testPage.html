Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that can be used by websites to make a user's experience more efficient.

Cookies are set through this website for a number of purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems.

Third-party cookies

Third-party cookies are not set by ISU. These are often included in embedded content such as news items or social media display tools. These are either there as part of the optimization of that service or as part of the delivery method for such embedded content (such as an embedded tweet). We also enable social media cookies on our website to allow you to share our content on social channels.

Performance

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our website.

Can I disable cookies?

Yes, although some of these electronic tools may need to collect and process your data in order to function correctly. If you choose to block these tools or switch them off, our website may not work correctly for you.
